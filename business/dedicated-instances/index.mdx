## Overview

Dedicated Instances provide a secure, private deployment model for ingesting, processing, and indexing data within a customer-controlled environment.

## Who This Is For

- Security and Compliance Teams  
- Platform and Infrastructure Teams  
- Application and Data Teams  

## Key Capabilities

- Private connectivity  
- Single-tenant isolation  
- Cloud-native integration  
- Scalable processing
