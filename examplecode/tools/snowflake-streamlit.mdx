


In your Snowflake account, on the sidebar, click **Projects > Streamlit**.
Click **+ Streamlit App**.
For **App title**, enter a name for your app, such as `Unstructured Demo Streamlit App`.
For **App location**, chose the target database and schema to store the app in.
For **App warehouse**, choose the warehouse that you want to use to run your app and execute its queries.
Click **Create**.
