To create the source connector:

1. On the sidebar, click **Sources**.
2. Click **New Source**.
3. In the **Type** drop-down list, select **Azure Blob Storage**.
4. Fill in the fields as described later on this page.
5. Click **Save and Test**.
6. Click **Close**.
